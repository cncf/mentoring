### KubeStellar

#### Community Building and User Success Manager

- Description: Build and nurture KubeStellar's user community while ensuring the first 10 production users are highly satisfied and successful. The mentee will establish community infrastructure including Slack or Discord, create community guidelines and onboarding processes, run weekly office hours, publish a weekly newsletter, conduct regular user check-ins, document feedback and feature requests, and develop a community champions program. This program emphasizes community management, customer success, empathy, and creating spaces where users help each other. The mentee may recruit 1-3 volunteer team members to assist with newsletter creation, office hours co-hosting, or user success coordination.
- Expected Outcome:
  - Active community platform with 50 engaged members
  - 12 weekly office hours sessions completed and recorded
  - 12 weekly newsletter editions published
  - Net Promoter Score above 8 from first 10 production users
  - 20 user testimonials collected and documented
  - Community playbook documenting programs and processes
  - 5 community champions identified and empowered
  - Evidence of users answering each other's questions
  - User feedback database with themes and priorities
  - User engagement: 15 GitHub issues filed by community members, 5 PRs or PR reviews contributed by community members
- Recommended Skills:
  - Community management or DevRel experience
  - Excellent written and verbal communication
  - Empathy and patience with users
  - Strong organizational and program management skills
  - Experience with community platforms like Slack or Discord
  - Customer success or support experience helpful

#### Content Creation and Developer Advocacy

- Description: Create high-quality technical content that drives inbound interest in KubeStellar and demonstrates its value to potential users. The mentee will develop and execute a content strategy including blog posts, video tutorials, technical deep-dives, and presentations at meetups or webinars. Content should address real user problems, showcase KubeStellar capabilities, and provide actionable guidance. This program emphasizes technical writing, video production, public speaking, and understanding what content resonates with the Kubernetes community. The mentee may recruit 1-3 volunteer team members to assist with video editing, social media management, or technical review.
- Expected Outcome:
  - 12 blog posts published across various platforms
  - 6 video tutorials covering key KubeStellar topics
  - 1-2 presentations delivered at meetups, webinars, or conferences
  - 1 technical deep-dive article of 2000+ words
  - 40 social media posts driving engagement and awareness
  - Content playbook documenting strategy and best practices
  - 5,000 total content views across all channels
  - Measurable inbound interest and signups attributed to content
  - User engagement: 10 GitHub issues filed by content-driven users, 3 PRs or PR reviews contributed by content-driven users
- Recommended Skills:
  - Strong technical writing and storytelling ability
  - Video production and editing skills
  - Kubernetes and cloud native expertise
  - Public speaking and presentation skills
  - Understanding of SEO and content distribution
  - Existing following or audience helpful but not required
